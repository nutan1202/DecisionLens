What happens when you click "Run Demo"?

  1. Keyword Generation: Extracts search keywords from your product title
  2. Candidate Search: Finds potential competitor products (mock data)

Each step is captured with full X-Ray traces showing inputs, outputs, reasoning, and candidate evaluations.
